About me

I am self taught in UX/UI design, having developed proficiency using tools such as Figma and Adobe Xd. Through portfolio projects I have also acquired knowledge and experience surrounding each aspect of the design process, such as user research, conceptual modelling, user flows and wireframing.

My knowledge further extends to Instructional Design. Through mentorship with the learning and development at the Co-op I am currently learning how to use various eLearning creation tools such as Storyline, Articulate & Rise 360, and Adobe Illustrator.

Discover Local Destinations Website

UX Design: A website aimed at ‘Helping People to Discover Local Destinations'. The problem at the heart of this design challenge is excessive foreign travel and thus with both climate change, and more recently the pandemic in mind I wanted to create a useable and innovative solution to the problem.
